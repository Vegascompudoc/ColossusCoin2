How it fits together

A pathway, not a funnel

STEP 01

Explore, free

Start with OpenCourseWare or the Open Learning Library — no cost, no clock.

STEP 02

Take a course

Move into a graded MITx course to test the material with real feedback.

STEP 03

Earn a credential

Stack courses into a MicroMasters, recognized toward an MIT Master's.

STEP 04

Apply it at work

Bring it back to the job through xPRO or MIT Horizon's workforce programs.
